Calculator

  • Project date: 02 June, 2023

This calculator app can cater to the different needs and preferences of the users. It is also designed to meet the needs of different users who require different levels of mathematical functionality. These functions can help them understand and explore different mathematical concepts and applications. Some users may want to this our app for practical or everyday purposes, such as budgeting, shopping, etc. These users may prefer having an ordinary calculator that can perform simple arithmetic operations, such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, fractions, percentages, etc. These operations can help them manage and calculate their finances and expenses. This app will also have a simple design that allows users to switch between the modes with ease and comfort. This app will be a useful and enjoyable tool that can support users in their mathematical endeavors and challenges.
